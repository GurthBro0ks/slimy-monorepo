## Context

Describe the forces at play, including technological, political, social, and project-local factors. These forces are likely in tension and should be called out as such. The language here is value-neutral; it's simply describing the facts.

## Decision

State the decision in full sentences, with active voice. "We will..." is a good starting phrase.

## Consequences

Describe the resulting context after applying the decision. All consequences should be listed here, not just the "positive" ones. A particular decision may have positive, negative, and neutral consequences, but all should be listed.
